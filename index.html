DBS Global University

DBS Global University strives to create a transformative educational experience focused on deep disciplinary knowledge through industry led experiential learning processes with adequate global exposure while promoting creativity, innovation through a comfortable living and fun experience.

  • Tech enablement in every subject and AI in every Business.
  • Choice based credit system to allow passion courses in each stream.
  • Essential credits of global skill certificates in each stream.
  • Globally benchmarked delivery through international faculty in online offline modes
  • Learning by empowering, the entire campus is managed by student committees.
  • Multiple internships and live projects in UG and PG programs.
  • Dehradun offers a fantastic living experience - a vibrant cosmopolitan city with buzzing student life. Allows for weekend getaways including trekking in the Himalayas and adventure sports.
  • The synergy of having a large no. of green industries and Agriculture & Forestry institutes in the vicinity makes live projects and learning easier.
